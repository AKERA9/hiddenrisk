What Might Break First When You Scale?

Supply Chain Scale Readiness Assessment

SECTION 1: SUPPLY STABILITY

1. Do you have more than one reliable source for your critical components?

2. Have you tested your supplier at 2x or 3x volume stress?

3. Can your supplier maintain consistency across large batches, not just samples?

4. If demand spikes suddenly, can your supply scale without delays?

SECTION 2: CONSISTENCY UNDER PRESSURE

5. Do you track batch-to-batch variation actively?

6. Have you ever faced “slight differences” that were ignored initially?

7. Are your current vendors capable of delivering identical performance repeatedly?

SECTION 3: SYSTEM VISIBILITY

8. Do you know what happens behind the product, not just the output?

9. Can you clearly identify where inconsistency may originate from?

10. Do you regularly audit your supply chain under real load conditions?

SECTION 4: SCALE READINESS

11. Has your system been tested for continuous high-volume production cycles?

12. Do you have buffer mechanisms in case of supply disruption?

13. Can your system handle growth without reducing control/visibility?

SECTION 5: EARLY WARNING SIGNALS

14. Have you noticed delays becoming slightly frequent?

15. Are there repeating issues with no clear root cause?

16. Do your teams rely more on past success than fresh validation?
